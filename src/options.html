New Key commands:

Up Arrow
Like photo. (Only works when in lightbox mode)
Down Arrow
Select comment text box. (Only works when in lightbox mode)
Backslash ( \ )
Automated loading of your followers. Copies a list of them to the clipboard.
Close Square Bracket ( ] )
Automated loading of users who you are following, but aren't following you back. Copies a list of them to the clipboard, and opens a new tab with links to their profiles.
